Analyze your Facebook activity

In order to analyze your Facebook activity, we need a Facebook token.
By providing us a Facebook Token, you agree to share with us certain information that we need to offer you our services.
The information provided doesn't violate the user's privacy, but nevertheless we promise that it will remain strictly confidential.
You can learn how to retrieve the Facebook token here.

The analyze should take up to 1 minute.

How to retrieve Facebook Token

To open Facebook Graph API, click on the link below:
https://developers.facebook.com/tools/explorer/
Log in on Facebook if not already logged in.


1. Click on Get Token

2. Click on Get User Access Token

3. Select email, user_likes, user_location and user_posts and click on Get Access Token

4. Confirm the action by clicking Okay

5. Copy the generated token and paste it in the Facebook Token field of this page
